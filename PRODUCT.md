# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, engineering managers, startup founders, and technical collaborators looking for an elite full-stack developer and AI specialist. They are scanning the web for candidates with high agency, solid engineering depth, and design taste.

## Product Purpose

Provide an immersive, highly professional, and unique digital space to showcase Norbert Brettschneider's (br3tt) projects, interactive AI demonstrations, and technical writing. The goal is to build authority, project trust, and establish a design-forward professional presence.

## Brand Personality

Professionalism, Passion, and Trust. Expressed through a creative, premium, and technically expressive visual language. Minimalist yet bespoke.

## Anti-references

- Banal three-column generic feature cards and template-bound grid layouts.
- Neon purple-blue "AI tool" gradient slop.
- Centered heroes over standard grid mesh backgrounds.
- Plain text logo wordmarks with no custom character.
- Fake dashboard screenshots or generic stock image blocks.

## Design Principles

1. **Show, Don't Tell**: Visual elements (like the interactive AI console and WebGL background) must function correctly, load fast, and be fully interactive.
2. **Gold and Obsidian Restraint**: Maintain the high-contrast luxury pairing of gold (`--accent`) and obsidian/chalk neutrals without adding unnecessary accent hues.
3. **Typography as Structure**: Rely on distinctive typography weights, line-height clearance, and clean line wraps instead of boxing everything in generic card containers.
4. **Snappy Motion**: Every interaction (button presses, scroll triggers) must feel responsive and tactile, respecting the user's focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Strict contrast checks for both dark and light modes, specifically avoiding white-on-gold overlay bugs.
- Viewport and layout stability (fixed-height terminal, min-h-[100dvh] dynamic sections).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with clean static or crossfade fallbacks for animations.
